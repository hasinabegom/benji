BENJI

In 1970, a kid from Texas pitched Hollywood a film about a little dog with a big heart. Every studio passed.

He and his wife produced the movie independently and then distributed it city to city, theater by theater.

It grossed $49 million. ($286 in today's dollars!)

Since then, over 1 billion people have watched the 12 Benji movies, TV specials and streamed films. All the while, that kid refused to ever sell his little mutt to Hollywood.
